Our Story

A decade of pursuit for the perfect crumb, the finest chocolate, and the most elegant designs. Welcome to the atelier where dreams are baked into reality.

Since 2014

The Pursuit of
Perfection

OVEN was born from a simple yet profound realization: that a cake is not merely a dessert, but a centerpiece of celebration, a canvas for artistry, and a memory in the making.

Founded by visionary pastry chef Elena Rossi, we began as a small boutique atelier in the heart of Manhattan. Today, we are a global name in luxury baking, serving royalty, icons, and those who simply appreciate the finer things in life.

Our Core Values

Uncompromising Quality

We source only the rarest ingredients: from Single-Origin Madagascar Vanilla to 24K edible gold leaves.

Artistic Excellence

Every stroke of frosting and every sugar petal is handcrafted by artisans with decades of experience.

Heartfelt Passion

We don't just bake; we care. Every client interaction is treated with the same precision as our recipes.
