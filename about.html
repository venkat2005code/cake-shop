Our Story
Of Artistry

We do not create food;
We create Permanent Memories.

At La Maison, we believe that every celebration is a blank canvas. Our mission is to fill that canvas with the fine-art of sweetness, using only the rarest ingredients sourced from the soul of the Earth.

Our Living History

1924

The First Atelier

Founded on Rue de Rivoli by Master Patissier Marc-André.

1952

Royal Commissions

La Maison becomes the official cake designer for European courts.

1988

Going Global

Expansion to New York and London flagships begins.

2024

The Digital Future

Launching our AI-driven bespoke design platform.
