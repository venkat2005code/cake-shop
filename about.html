THE HERITAGE

Our Story
Of Artistry

We do not create food;
We create Permanent Memories.

At OVEN, we believe that every celebration is a unique blueprint. Our mission is to architect memories through the ancient alchemy of fire and fine-art patisserie, using only the rarest ingredients sourced from the soul of the Earth.

Our Living History

1924

The First Hearth

Founded in a small village atelier, centering the bakery around a hand-built stone hearth.

1952

Royal Commissions

OVEN becomes the official cake designer for European courts.

1988

Going Global

Expansion to New York and London flagships begins.

2024

The Digital Future

Launching our AI-driven bespoke design platform.
