Connoisseur's FAQ

What is the typical lead time for a Couture Wedding?

We recommend initiating the Commission Brief at least 6 to 9 months in advance. For destination weddings, 12 months is preferred to ensure logistical perfection.

Do you offer global shipping for bespoke cakes?

Yes. We operate a private, temperature-controlled fleet for local deliveries and utilize specialized climate-controlled air freight for international commissions.

Can I request a personalized flavor landscape?

Absolutely. During your Private Tasting at the atelier, we work with you to map ingredients from your own history—honey from your estate, or botanical infusions found in your childhood garden.

Is the Private Residency available globally?

The Residency program is available in North America, Europe, and Asia. For locations outside these regions, additional logistical planning is required.
