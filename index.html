FAQ

Common Inquiries

How far in advance should I order?

For bespoke wedding cakes, we recommend 4-6 months. For signature collection cakes, a minimum of 72 hours is required for preparation.

Do you deliver internationally?

Currently, we deliver across Paris, New York, and London via our temperature-controlled fleet to ensure texture preservation.

Are there vegan options?

Our 'Botanical Range' features 100% plant-based masterpieces without compromising on the signature OVEN silkiness.

Can I request a tasting?

Absolutely. We host private tasting soirées at our flagship ateliers every Wednesday and Saturday. Reservation is required.
